# Cart GUI

The shopping cart GUI allows you to manage multiple items before purchasing them all at once.

## Accessing the Cart

Open your cart using:
```
/ah cart
```

Or click the cart button in the main auction house GUI.

## GUI Layout

### Cart Items
- **Item Display**: Shows all items in your cart
- **Item Details**: Name, price, seller for each item
- **Remove Button**: Remove items from cart
- **Total Cost**: Sum of all item prices

### Action Buttons
- **Checkout**: Purchase all items at once
- **Clear Cart**: Remove all items from cart
- **Back**: Return to main auction house

## Adding Items to Cart

To add items to your cart:
1. Browse the auction house with `/ah`
2. Click on an item you want to buy
3. Click "Add to Cart" instead of "Buy Now"
4. Item is added to your cart
5. Repeat for more items

## Cart Features

### Item Management
- **View Items**: See all items in your cart
- **Remove Items**: Click items to remove them
- **Item Details**: View full item information
- **Price Display**: See individual and total prices

### Checkout Process
1. **Review Items**: Check all items in cart
2. **Verify Total**: Ensure you have enough money
3. **Click Checkout**: Purchase all items
4. **Receive Items**: All items are delivered

## Cart Information

### Displayed Information
- **Item Name**: Name of each item
- **Item Price**: Price of each item
- **Seller**: Who is selling each item
- **Total Cost**: Sum of all prices
- **Item Count**: Number of items in cart

## Auto-Refresh

The cart GUI auto-refreshes if enabled:
- **Real-time Updates**: Cart updates when items change
- **Price Updates**: Prices update if they change
- **Availability**: Items removed if they sell

## Cart Limitations

### Item Availability
- Items may sell before you checkout
- Cart items are not reserved
- Sold items are removed automatically

### Money Requirements
- Must have enough money for all items
- Partial checkout is not supported
- All items purchased or none

### Expiration
- Cart persists across sessions
- Items may expire while in cart
- Expired items are removed

## Tips

1. **Add Multiple Items** - Build up your cart before purchasing
2. **Check Total** - Ensure you have enough money
3. **Act Quickly** - Items may sell before checkout
4. **Review Items** - Check items before purchasing
5. **Use for Bulk Buying** - Great for buying many items

## Related Commands

- `/ah cart` - Open your cart
- `/ah` - Browse and add items to cart
- `/ah transactions` - View purchase history
