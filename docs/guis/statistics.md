# Statistics GUIs

The statistics GUIs provide comprehensive statistics about auction house activity.

## Accessing the GUI

Open statistics using:
```
/ah stats
```

Or view another player's stats:
```
/ah stats <player>
```

## Statistics Selection GUI

When first opened, you can choose:
- **Your Statistics**: View your own stats
- **Other Player**: View another player's stats
- **Leaderboards**: View server leaderboards
- **Category Selection**: Choose stat category

## Statistics Display

### Overall Statistics
- **Total Sales**: Number of items sold
- **Total Purchases**: Number of items bought
- **Total Earnings**: Money earned
- **Total Spent**: Money spent
- **Net Profit**: Profit or loss
- **Average Prices**: Average sale/purchase prices

### Sales Statistics
- **Sales Count**: Number of sales
- **Sales Value**: Total sales value
- **Highest Sale**: Highest sale price
- **Lowest Sale**: Lowest sale price
- **Average Sale**: Average sale price
- **Most Sold Item**: Item sold most often

### Purchase Statistics
- **Purchase Count**: Number of purchases
- **Purchase Value**: Total purchase value
- **Highest Purchase**: Highest purchase price
- **Lowest Purchase**: Lowest purchase price
- **Average Purchase**: Average purchase price
- **Most Purchased Item**: Item bought most often

### Earnings Statistics
- **Total Earnings**: Total money earned
- **Sales Earnings**: Earnings from sales
- **Auction Earnings**: Earnings from auctions
- **BIN Earnings**: Earnings from BIN
- **Payment Status**: Payment collection status

## Leaderboards

### Available Leaderboards
- **Top Sellers**: Players with most sales
- **Top Buyers**: Players with most purchases
- **Highest Earners**: Players who earned most
- **Most Active**: Most active users
- **Best Deals**: Best deals made

### Leaderboard Display
- **Rankings**: Player rankings
- **Values**: Statistic values
- **Comparison**: Compare with others
- **Your Position**: Your ranking

## Statistics Features

### Visual Display
- **Charts**: Graphical representations
- **Graphs**: Visual data display
- **Comparisons**: Compare with others
- **Trends**: Historical trends

### Navigation
- **Switch Categories**: Change stat types
- **View Leaderboards**: Access leaderboards
- **Compare Players**: Compare statistics
- **Export Data**: Export statistics (if available)

## Tips

1. **Track Performance** - Monitor your activity
2. **Set Goals** - Use stats to set goals
3. **Compare** - Compare with other players
4. **Analyze Trends** - See patterns in activity
5. **Use Leaderboards** - Compete with others

## Related Commands

- `/ah stats` - View statistics
- `/ah transactions` - View transaction history
- `/ah active` - View active listings
